Why inventor?

When I was a kid, I wanted to be a soldier, then an entertainer, and then an inventor. Young me wanted to do these because he wanted to make people happy. For the future, regardless of what I decide to do, I want to remember that it's still just young Faiyaz just wanting to make people happy. The ends don't justify the means. I don't want to become the next Tech Villain.
