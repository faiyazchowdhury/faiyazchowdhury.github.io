Music

My favorite artists are Ed Sheeran (I can play like all his songs lol), Secret Garden, Radiohead, Muse, Sting, ABBA, and any soundtrack composer. I mainly play the guitar, but I like to compose on the piano, and improvise blues on the saxophone, and some Ocarina lol. Favorite songs of all time... hmm... Take On Me by A-ha, Afire Love and What Do I Know by Ed Sheeran, Fields of Gold by Sting, My Way by Frank Sinatra and A Million Dreams from The Greatest Showman. Favorite albums to the left. Classical Pieces to Practice

With classical guitar, I like to play by ear. It gives me a deeper understanding of the music when I try to reinvent it myself. I like happy songs in D major. It gives a very natural almost irish folk sound. I'm not the type that learns popular or difficult songs. I do this for fun, so it should be things I like. A lot of these are performed by Alexandra Whittingham. I adore her!
